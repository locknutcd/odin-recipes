Roasted lamb shank

The most scrumdiddlyumptious shank of the lamb and how to prepare it for much yum

Ingredients:

  • 1 shank of de lamb

Steps

  1. Place lamb shank in oven tray
  2. Set over to bake at at 220 degrees celcius
  3. Bake lamb shank for 50 minutes until golden brown
